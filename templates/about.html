Frequently asked Questions.
Why yet another platform for interview questions?
We know the internet is filled with the interview experiences and stuffs related to it but still a lots of universities maintains a personal repository of the interviewing experiences of their students it not only helps to figure out the companies visiting on campus, also it helps where you need to go a mile ahead with respect to the normal college curriculum and how your peers approached companies, off campus.
Is it ethical/legal to share interview questions?
Yes and no. It's generally a yes for most of the companies except a few where you need to sign a bond for not doing so. However signing a NDA for interview questions is extremely rare.
Can I share an experience on behalf of my friend?
Technically you can do so. But we highly encourage not doing that because, the chances of errors increases and thereby the quality of the content decreases.
